About

The full picture,
built from scratch.

Article One maps the complete financial universe of individual Members of Congress — campaign committees, leadership PACs, office spending, outside groups, and personal financial disclosures — assembled from primary sources and cross-referenced against each other.

Most congressional finance tools show you one entity at a time. Article One shows you the whole network: who's being paid, across which committees, for how long, and what that pattern means.

Built on FEC filings, House Disbursement records, FPPC data, and financial disclosures. Every figure is traceable to its source — no hand-typed numbers.
