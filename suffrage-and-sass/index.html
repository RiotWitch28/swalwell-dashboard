Suffrage & Sass is an independent studio making civic technology that anyone can use. The work sits at the edge of data journalism, political science, and AI — with a design sensibility that insists on clarity over complexity.

Every project starts with one question: who benefits from this staying opaque? Then we build the opposite of that.

Powered by FEC data, Congressional records, PACER filings, and a conviction that democracy works better when people can see the machine.
